About Neko Charvanni

Neko Charvanni is a sensual storyteller and independent author crafting immersive LGBTQ+ paranormal romance with heart, heat, and humanity. Blending poetic prose with vivid worldbuilding, her stories explore love, identity, and transformation through strong femme-centered characters, mystical lineages, and emotionally raw journeys.

Rooted in a deep respect for cultural heritage and spiritual evolution, Neko’s work often weaves elements of African mythology, nature-based symbolism, and queer empowerment into richly layered narratives. Her signature style balances passion and vulnerability, drawing readers into emotionally intense stories that linger long after the final page.

When she’s not writing, Neko can be found studying languages, designing websites, knitting elaborate blankets, or cooking up business dreams in the kitchen. She’s a firm believer in self-taught mastery, storytelling as activism, and building a creative life on her own terms.

Whether you're here for the soulmates, the spice, or the supernatural — welcome to Neko's world.
